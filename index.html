Citronics provide the right platform for students to showcase their prowess and compete not only at the state level but also with students at national level.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

FAQ's

What is the Techno Management Feast?

The feast converges technology and management, offering insights for diverse audiences. Explore cutting-edge trends, network with experts, and gain valuable knowledge at this dynamic event.

Who should attend?

Tailored for tech enthusiasts, business leaders, and students, the feast caters to a diverse audience. Whether a novice or expert, discover valuable insights and networking opportunities.

What to expect from sessions?

Engage with industry leaders through keynotes, panels, and workshops. Sessions cover emerging technologies, digital transformation, leadership, and more, providing actionable insights and best practices.

How to get involved?

Participate as an attendee for access to all sessions, submit a speaking proposal, or explore sponsorship opportunities. Stay updated on the website and social media channels for details.
